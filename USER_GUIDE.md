# GroSphere: User Operation Manual

Welcome to **GroSphere**, your AI-powered mission control for urban gardening. This guide will walk you through the primary features and operations of the platform.

---

## 1. Getting Started
- **Account Creation:** Launch the app and sign up using your email. This creates your secure profile in the GroSphere cloud.
- **Onboarding:** Upon first login, the app will request permissions for **Camera** (for AI Scanning/AR) and **Location** (for weather-adjusted watering schedules).

## 2. Mission Control (Dashboard)
The Dashboard is your central hub for real-time telemetry.
- **Ecosystem Health:** Monitor the overall status of your garden at a glance.
- **Active Flora:** View your primary specimens. Tapping any specimen card will take you to its **Advanced Analytics**.
- **IoT Nodes:** View live status from your connected sensors (Soil Moisture, Light, Temp).

## 3. Advanced Analytics & Growth Charts
- **Accessing Data:** Tap on any plant in the "Primary Specimens" section.
- **Visualizing History:** View time-series charts for moisture, light, and temperature.
- **AI Projections:** Read the AI-generated "Growth Velocity" and "Vitality Score" to predict when your plant will reach its next growth phase.

## 4. AI Scanner & Diagnostics
- **Launch:** Tap the **AI Scanner** button on the Dashboard.
- **Identification:** Point your camera at a plant and tap the scan icon. The AI will identify the species with high confidence.
- **Health Check:** The scanner also detects common diseases and pests. If an issue is found, the **Gardening Copilot** will offer immediate organic treatment suggestions.

## 5. AR Smart Guidance
- **Launch:** Tap **AR Guidance** from the Dashboard or a specific plant's detail page.
- **Modes:**
    - **Pruning Mode:** Follow the holographic cut-lines (Structural, Deadhead, or Harvest) to prune your plants like a pro.
    - **Measuring Mode:** Drag the virtual markers (A & B) to measure stem height or leaf width. Metrics are saved directly to the plant's log.
    - **Scanning Mode:** Use multi-spectral AR callouts to see real-time "tags" appearing over identified plant features.

## 6. Community & Seed Swap
- **Global Feed:** Share your garden progress with other urban gardeners.
- **Seed Swap:** Visit the **Marketplace** to list your own seed stock for trade or browse listings from others.
- **Achievements:** Earn badges like "Carbon Warrior" or "Master Harvester" as you grow.

## 7. Gardening Copilot (AI Chat)
- **Direct Link:** Tap **Open Intelligence** on the Dashboard.
- **Query:** Ask anything from "Why are my tomato leaves yellowing?" to "What should I plant in my north-facing balcony in June?"
- **Context:** The Copilot knows your local weather and the current status of your specimens, providing highly personalized advice.

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips
- **Sync Status:** Check the "System Online" indicator in the header. If it pulses red, check your network connection for real-time telemetry updates.
- **Watering Reminders:** The app automatically pauses reminders if the **AI Copilot** detects heavy rain in your local forecast.
